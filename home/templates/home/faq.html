Resseting your password

To change the password for your account while you are already logged in, go to "profile" then click "Update profile", change your password in the password section and click "Update".

Discover Posts

Who can see my top posts?

Anyone of Inscribe can see your top posts when they view your blog(unless you have hidden your posts in which case no one except you can see it.

Playground

This is an enticing feature provided in Inscribe which allows you to analyze your sentiments at the time based on the content you write. With the help of artificial intelligence(AI) your notes are processed and one out of three results (🙂 when positive , 😐 when neutral , ☹ when negative) will appear. This enables you to try out this amazing feature without actually drafting a post.
